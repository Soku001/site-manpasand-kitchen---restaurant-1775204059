Our Culinary Journey

Mouth-Watering Menu Highlights

Lahori Chargha

Our signature whole roasted chicken, marinated overnight in traditional Lahori spices and slow-cooked to juicy perfection.

Gosht Karahi

Tender pieces of mutton cooked in a rich, spicy tomato-ginger gravy, a true Pakistani comfort food masterpiece.

Chicken Biryani

Fragrant basmati rice layered with succulent chicken, infused with saffron, mint, and secret spices for an unforgettable aroma.

Peshawari Chapli Kebab

Authentic flat minced meat patties, seasoned with pomegranate seeds and green chilies, grilled to smoky perfection.

Gulab Jamun

Soft, melt-in-your-mouth milk solids dumplings, deep-fried and soaked in a fragrant rose and cardamom syrup.

The Manpasand Story

Manpasand Kitchen & Restaurant was born from a passion to bring the vibrant, rich flavors of Pakistani cuisine to the heart of Abu Dhabi. Our journey began with a simple philosophy: use the freshest ingredients, time-honored recipes, and a touch of heartfelt hospitality to create an unparalleled dining experience.

Every dish tells a story, a heritage of generations of culinary artistry. From the bustling streets of Lahore to the serene valleys of Peshawar, our chefs meticulously craft each meal, ensuring an authentic taste that transports you to Pakistan. We invite you to discover the true essence of Manpasand – "the beloved choice."
